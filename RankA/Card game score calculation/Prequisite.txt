Problem
12 cards are given with numbers written on them.
Make 4 groups of 3.
For each group
1 point, if they are orderly.
2 points, if they are the same number.
0 point, if neither of the above.
Find the maximum possible score.  

There will only be a maximum 5 cards of the same number.


Input Example 1
1 1 1 2 2 2 3 3 3 4 4 4

Output Example 1
8

Input Example 2
1 2 3 4 5 6 7 7 7 8 9 9

Output Example 2
4

Problem
Number of rows and columns of boards are given.
Each board has a score.
Knockdown from the topmost board.
The knocked board will in turn knock down the directly below or left or right,
which in turn trigger a chain reaction.
For scoring, add individual scores of each knocked board 
Find the maximum possible score.

Input Example
3 4
1 1 2 1
2 1 1 2
2 1 1 2

Output Example
6


Input Example
4 4
5 4 3 10
1 3 0 6
9 0 3 2
3 5 1 3

Output Example
24

Problem
For each given book, there will be number of pages and time taken to complete them.
1st line gives the number of books and time limit
The rest are books with page numbers and time taken.
Find the maximum number of pages read for the time constraint.

Input Example 1
3 15
300 7
250 7
310 6

Output Example 1
610

Input Example 2
2 30
590 14
900 15

Output Example 2
1490

Problem
Player count, Word List and Play Words are given.
Rules
1. The Play word must be in the Word List
2. The First Alphabet must be Last Alphabet of the previous Play Word
3. Previously used words can't be used again
4. Can't use a word which ends in 'z'
If any rules is broken, the player is forfeited. 

Print out the remaining number of Players and player list in ascending order



Input Example 1
3 6 7
a
aloha
app
az
paiza
warp
app
paiza
a
aloha
az
warp
paiza

Output Example 1
1
3

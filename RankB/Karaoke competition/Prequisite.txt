Problem
Number of competitors and the key points are given.
Correct pitches and the sung pitches are given.
Each competitor will be given 100 points at first.
No point will be deducted if the pitch difference is within 5db.
1 point will be deducted if the pitch difference is within 10db.
2 points will be deducted if the pitch difference is within 20db.
3 points will be deducted if the pitch difference is within 30db.
5 points will be deducted if the pitch difference is above 30db.

Find the maximum score of the winner.


Input Example
2 3
400
410
420
400
400
400
300
300
300

Output Example
97
